FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ)

ACCOUNT MANAGEMENT
Q: How do I open a new bank account?
A: You can open a new account by asking me about the specific requirements and process. I can guide you through the documentation needed, minimum deposits, and account types available. Feel free to ask me any questions about opening different types of accounts.

Q: What is the minimum balance requirement?
A: Our standard checking account requires a minimum balance of $100 to avoid monthly maintenance fees. Savings accounts require a minimum of $25. Premium accounts may have higher requirements.

Q: How can I check my account balance?
A: You can check your balance by asking me directly. I can help you with balance inquiries and guide you through different ways to access your account information. Just ask me about your current balance or account details.

Q: Can I have multiple accounts?
A: Yes, you can open multiple accounts including checking, savings, money market, and certificates of deposit (CDs). Each account type serves different financial goals.

ONLINE BANKING & MOBILE APP
Q: How do I enroll in online banking?
A: I can guide you through the enrollment process step by step. Ask me about the requirements for setting up online access, what information you'll need, and how to get started with digital banking services.

Q: Is online banking secure?
A: Yes, we use industry-standard encryption, multi-factor authentication, and fraud monitoring to protect your accounts. Never share your login credentials with anyone.

Q: Can I deposit checks through the mobile app?
A: Yes, you can deposit checks using mobile check deposit. Simply take a photo of the front and back of the endorsed check. Funds are typically available within 1-2 business days.

Q: What should I do if I forget my password?
A: I can help you with password recovery options and security procedures. Ask me about the steps to reset your password, security verification methods, and account recovery processes.

DEBIT & CREDIT CARDS
Q: How long does it take to receive a new debit card?
A: New debit cards are typically mailed within 5-7 business days. You can request expedited shipping for an additional fee. You can also pick up a temporary card at most branches.

Q: What should I do if my card is lost or stolen?
A: I can guide you through the immediate steps to protect your account. Ask me about card security procedures, how to report lost cards, replacement processes, and fraud protection measures.

Q: What are the daily withdrawal limits?
A: Standard daily ATM withdrawal limit is $500, and daily purchase limit is $3,000. If you need higher limits, ask me about the process to request limit increases and what options are available.

Q: Are there fees for using my debit card?
A: There are no fees for purchases made with your debit card. However, ATM fees may apply when using non-network ATMs. We reimburse up to $10 in out-of-network ATM fees per month for premium accounts.

TRANSFERS & PAYMENTS
Q: How do I set up automatic bill pay?
A: Log into online banking, navigate to "Bill Pay," and add your payees. You can schedule one-time or recurring payments. Most payments are processed within 1-2 business days.

Q: Can I transfer money to other banks?
A: Yes, you can transfer funds to external accounts through online banking or the mobile app. Transfers typically take 1-3 business days. Wire transfers are available for same-day transfers (fees apply).

Q: What is Zelle and how do I use it?
A: Zelle is a fast, safe way to send money to friends and family using their email or phone number. Funds are typically available within minutes. You can access Zelle through our mobile app or online banking.

INTEREST RATES & FEES
Q: What interest rate does my savings account earn?
A: Our standard savings account currently earns 0.50% APY. High-yield savings accounts earn up to 4.25% APY depending on balance. Rates are subject to change and may vary.

Q: What are the monthly maintenance fees?
A: Standard checking accounts have a $12 monthly fee, which is waived if you maintain a $1,500 minimum daily balance, have direct deposits totaling $500+ per month, or are a student. Premium accounts may have different fee structures.

Q: Are there overdraft fees?
A: Yes, overdraft fees are $35 per item. We offer overdraft protection by linking your savings account or a line of credit. You can also opt out of overdraft coverage for debit card transactions.

LOANS & CREDIT
Q: How do I apply for a personal loan?
A: You can apply online, through the mobile app, or at a branch. You'll need proof of income, employment verification, and credit information. Approval decisions are typically made within 24 hours.

Q: What is the interest rate on personal loans?
A: Personal loan rates range from 6.99% to 18.99% APR depending on creditworthiness, loan amount, and term length. Rates are fixed for the life of the loan.

Q: Can I refinance my existing loan?
A: Yes, you may be eligible to refinance your loan to get a better rate or adjust your payment terms. Contact our lending department to discuss your options.

INVESTMENT SERVICES
Q: Do you offer investment advisory services?
A: Yes, we have licensed financial advisors who can help with retirement planning, portfolio management, and investment strategies. Initial consultations are complimentary.

Q: What investment products are available?
A: We offer mutual funds, ETFs, stocks, bonds, IRAs, 401(k) rollovers, and managed portfolios. Minimum investment amounts vary by product.

SECURITY & FRAUD
Q: How do I protect myself from fraud?
A: Never share your account information, passwords, or PINs. Monitor your accounts regularly, set up account alerts, and review statements. Report suspicious activity immediately.

Q: What should I do if I notice unauthorized transactions?
A: Contact us immediately. We'll investigate and may issue a temporary credit while we review. You're protected by our zero-liability policy for unauthorized debit card transactions when reported promptly.

Q: How does the bank protect my information?
A: We use advanced encryption, secure servers, and strict privacy policies. We never sell your personal information to third parties. We comply with all federal privacy regulations.

OTHER SERVICES
Q: Do you offer notary services?
A: Yes, notary services are available at most branches free of charge for account holders. Please call ahead to ensure a notary is available.

Q: Can I order checks?
A: Yes, you can order checks through online banking, by phone, or at a branch. Standard delivery takes 7-10 business days. Custom checks may take longer.

Q: What are your branch hours?
A: Most branches are open Monday-Friday 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, and Saturday 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM. Some locations have extended hours. Drive-through services may have different hours.

Q: How do I update my contact information?
A: You can update your address, phone number, or email through online banking, the mobile app, by calling customer service, or visiting a branch. Always keep your information current for security purposes.

Q: Do you offer student accounts?
A: Yes, we offer special student checking accounts with no monthly fees, no minimum balance requirements, and free ATM transactions. Students must provide proof of enrollment.

ADDITIONAL ASSISTANCE
For any banking questions or assistance, simply ask me! I'm here to help with all your banking needs, from account information to transaction assistance. Feel free to ask me anything about your accounts, services, or banking procedures.
